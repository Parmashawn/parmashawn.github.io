FAQ

How do I use this site?

It's pretty simple. Just type in the number of each kind of fish or gemstone into the calculator, and the website will automatically give you the amount of gems, world locks, and byte coins they are worth. You can factor in your clan level bonus or the rate at which you are selling gems in the options tab.
