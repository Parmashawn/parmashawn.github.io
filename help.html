FAQ

How to Install Pixelworlds Gems?

PWGems.com is availible as a web app on IOS and Android with just a few taps!

Android

  1. Go to www.pwgems.com
  2. Open your browser settings
  3. Tap Add to Home screen

IOS

  1. Go to www.pwgems.com
  2. Press the share button
  3. Tap Add to Home screen
What is PWGems?
Pixelworlds Gems is a free gem calculating site mainly made for calculating gems. However the website does have other features and pixelworlds related expirimental programs availible. In general it is a tool for Pixelworlds Players
